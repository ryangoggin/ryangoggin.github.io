About Me

Hi and thanks for checking out my portfolio site! My name is Ryan Goggin, I'm a software engineer who graduated from App Academy's full-time Software Engineering Program in April 2023, and I'm excited to use the skills I've learned and cultivated to become a full-time SWE in the near future. I have a passion for working with JavaScript (React/Redux) and Python (Flask/SQLAlchemy), but am excited to learn more languages and technologies as I start working. With what I've learned already I've been capable of making 3 full-stack clone projects: GoggInn (an Airbnb clone), PixelPal (a Discord clone), and Goggbook (a Facebook clone). You can find out more about these projects if you scroll down to the Projects section!

Aside from coding and software engineering, I also graduated from Brown University in May 2020 with an Sc.B. in Chemical Engineering and worked in pharmaceuticals and biotechnology (AstraZeneca and Lonza) for two years in the Bay Area prior to switching careers. In my free time I also love to travel, see my friends, hike, ski, go out for brunch, and I'm starting to get back into reading. Definitely reach out to me if you think I'd be a good fit on your team or even if you just want to chat about my background, cool travel/ski/hiking/brunch spots, or book recs!

Projects

GoggInn

GoggInn is a full-stack clone of Airbnb that can be used to view and review spots, post and manage your own spots, and book visits to other user's spots.

Stack: JavaScript, React, Redux, Express.js, Sequelize, HTML/CSS, PostgreSQL, Render

  • Live Site
  • Github Repo

PixelPal

PixelPal is a full-stack clone based off Discord that can be used to create servers with channels to talk to and interact with other users in your communities in real time.

Stack: JavaScript, React, Redux, Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, HTML/CSS, PostgreSQL, Socket.io, Render

  • Live Site
  • Github Repo

Goggbook

Goggbook is a full-stack clone of Facebook that can be used to post your thoughts and images to your friends, like and comment on your friends' posts, view other user's profile pages, and edit your bio and profile picture from your own profile page.

Stack: JavaScript, React, Redux, Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, HTML/CSS, PostgreSQL, AWS S3, Render
